# Chronos Timekeeping — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 13, 2026
**Last Updated:** June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Jordan P. Ehrig, Sr.**, sole proprietor doing business as
**Chronos Timekeeping** ("we," "us," "our"), collects, uses, and shares information in
connection with the Chronos Timekeeping software, website (chronos-timekeeping.com), and
related services (the "Service"). It is part of, and incorporated into, the Terms of Service &
EULA.

> **Stripe and other payment processors require a published privacy policy. Replace the
> bracketed placeholders and have this reviewed before going live.**

## 1. Privacy-First, Local-First Design

Chronos is designed to keep your activity data **on your own device.** Time Data — including
file-path metadata, window/process information, project classifications, timestamps, and
durations — is stored locally in an encrypted database (AES-256-GCM) on your machine. **We do
not collect the contents of your files;** Chronos records metadata (such as file paths and
durations) used to classify time, not document contents.

## 2. Information We Collect

**(a) Information you provide:**
- Account and contact details (name, email) when you register, purchase, or contact support.
- Billing information processed by Stripe (see Section 4) — we do **not** receive or store
  your full payment-card number.
- Communications you send us (support requests, feedback).

**(b) Information collected automatically:**
- **Local activity data (Time Data)** generated on your device, stored locally as described
  above. This stays on your device unless you explicitly export, sync, or transmit it.
- **Telemetry / diagnostic data** — limited operational, crash, version, and feature-usage
  data, where enabled, used to maintain and improve the Service. You may be able to disable
  telemetry in settings where offered.
- **Website data** — standard log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) and cookies
  or similar technologies used to operate the site and measure traffic.

**(c) Information from third parties:**
- Limited transaction and subscription status data from Stripe (e.g., whether a payment
  succeeded), not full card data.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to: (a) provide, operate, secure, and maintain the Service; (b) process
payments and manage subscriptions; (c) provide support and respond to inquiries; (d) improve
and develop features (including aggregated or de-identified analysis); (e) detect, prevent,
and address fraud, abuse, security, and technical issues; and (f) comply with legal
obligations and enforce our terms. We do **not sell** your personal information, and we do not
use your local Time Data for advertising.

## 4. Payment Processing (Stripe)

Payments are processed by **Stripe, Inc.**, an independent third-party processor. When you
make a purchase, your payment details are collected and processed by Stripe under Stripe's own
privacy policy and terms. We receive transaction metadata (such as confirmation, amount, and
subscription status) but not your full card number. See https://stripe.com/privacy.

## 5. Monitored Persons — Customer as Controller

If you use Chronos to monitor, record, or classify the activity or files of any person other
than yourself ("Monitored Persons" — e.g., employees or contractors), **you are the data
controller for that data, and you are responsible** for providing required notices, obtaining
required consents, and complying with all applicable monitoring, wiretap, employment, and
privacy laws. We process such data, if at all, only as a service provider/processor acting on
your instructions and on your behalf.

## 6. How We Share Information

We share information only: (a) with service providers who help us operate the Service (e.g.,
Stripe for payments, hosting and email providers), under confidentiality obligations; (b) to
comply with law, legal process, or lawful government requests; (c) to protect our rights,
safety, and property, or that of others, and to enforce our terms; and (d) in connection with
a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, with notice as required by law. We do
not sell or rent personal information.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain account and transaction information for as long as your account is active and as
needed to provide the Service, comply with legal and tax obligations, resolve disputes, and
enforce agreements. **Local Time Data is retained on your device under your control** and is
deleted when you delete it or uninstall the Software (subject to your own backups).

## 8. Security

We use technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption of
local Time Data (AES-256-GCM). **However, no method of transmission or storage is completely
secure,** and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for safeguarding your
device, credentials, and backups.
