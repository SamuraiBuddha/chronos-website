Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

Pro subscriptions are billed monthly or annually. Annual subscribers save 17% compared to the monthly rate. Enterprise is billed per seat per month with volume discounts available for teams of 10 or more.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your local database stays on your machine. Chronos never stores your data on our servers, so there is nothing to delete. Your full history remains accessible to you indefinitely.

Is there a free tier?

We are evaluating a limited free tier with basic window-title tracking and up to 3 projects. In the meantime, contact us at sales@chronos-timekeeping.com to discuss your situation.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Chronos works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed for license activation, optional AI model downloads, and Enterprise sync features.

What's included in Enterprise that isn't in Pro?

Enterprise adds team management: admin dashboards, approval workflows, centralized cross-team reporting, bidirectional sync between machines, accounting integrations (QuickBooks, FreshBooks), and priority support. Every detection and tracking feature is identical between plans.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Pro to Enterprise or adjust seat count at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
